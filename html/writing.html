EXT. THE GLADE – DAY

Sunlight streams through towering ivy-covered walls. The Glade stretches out like a paradise prison - lush gardens, makeshift shelters, and boys working the land. In the center, a crude elevator shaft - THE BOX - sits ominously quiet.

THOMAS (16) wakes with a gasp. Confusion clouds his eyes. He scrambles to his feet, spinning around, taking in the impossible walls surrounding them.

THOMAS

Where... where am I?

ALBY (17), strong and weathered beyond his years, approaches with cautious authority. Behind him, a crowd of GLADERS gather, watching the newcomer with a mix of curiosity and wariness.

ALBY

Welcome to the Glade, Greenie.

Thomas stumbles backward, eyes wild with panic.

THOMAS

What is this place? How did I get here?

ALBY

Same way we all did. The Box brought you. That's all you need to know for now.

In the distance, a GRINDING SOUND echoes. Thomas's head snaps toward massive doors in the wall - THE MAZE ENTRANCE - slowly opening, revealing darkness beyond.

THOMAS

What's out there?

Alby's expression darkens.

ALBY

The Maze. And you don't want to go in there, trust me.
